About Us

Report and Advocacy.

We are startup youth iniative based in Kigali aimed at helping combating domestic violance in Rwanda as else where in the world. we are a non-profit organisation under private federation, on Report and Advocy our clients report cases for support in case related to Domestic violance and we find quick support from RIB and Isange one stop center as we push harder for quick fix.

Aid and Support.

We gather Funds and support from individuals and charity organisaion to help the victims of domestic as well as intimate partner violance.
we believe that being needy doesn't mean being weak, victms will have access to claim support. support to needy people will be offered after only abuse has validity of 3 months with avalid proof .

Conflict Resolution and Teaching.

Our trained team organises meeting with one of the listed villages and explain more about domestic violance in so doing the habitants get the chances to report cases, ask questions as well as get delivered with support on other issues regarding violance .
